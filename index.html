Welcome to CSIT

Course Information

Estimated Class Length: 900 hours

Earn the following credentials: CompTIA A+, CompTIA Network+, CompTIA Security+

First part of the course "Computer Systems Technician" (300 hours)

  1. Class training with personal computer hardware, operating systems, and software. troubleshooting operating systems, including word processing, databases, reports, spreadsheets, multimedia presentations, electronic calendar, contacts, email, and internet applications; and customer relations, communication skills, and employability skills.

Second Part of the Course "Computer Network Technician" (150 hours)

  1. Training includes networking protocols, understanding the Open Systems Interconnection (OSI) model, implementing a Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN), and hardware and software security devices.

Third Part of the Course "Computer Networking Specialist" (150 hours)

    Training includes basic routing concepts; and implement, verify, and troubleshoot Network Address Translation (NAT) and Access Control Lists (ACL) in a medium-size enterprise branch office network.

Fourth Part of Course Computer Security Technician (300 hours)

  1. Training includes an understanding of cybersecurity, the terminology used, its history and culture, and trends, dealing with threats, viruses and malware.

Graduation Requirements

  1. Minimum Grade required to pass: 70%
  2. Minimum Attendance Required: 85%
  3. Student's competency in computation (mathematics) and communication (reading/language arts) is assessed; required basic skills grade levels are: Computation - 9 and Communication - 9.

Additional Information

  1. Class fixes computers if someone in school has an issue with their personal computer
  2. Good amount of hands on learning
  3. Lots of training with operating systems via virtual machines
  4. Self paced environment with potential to get ahead
